The Spark

Microbots began with a clear challenge: Syrian engineering students graduate with strong theory but little exposure to real industrial systems. This gap limits their ability to contribute to the country‚Äôs economic recovery.

One volunteer took action‚Äîand that effort grew into a movement of more than 60 committed volunteers providing hands-on training, mentorship, and real engineering experience to young talent across Syria.

Our Evolution

Community workshops expanded into structured programs and industry collaborations. As demand grew, Microbots transitioned into a formal NGO led by a core team of 10 specialists focused on long-term, measurable impact.

Multi-Stage Technical Training

Short, intensive workshops co-designed with companies to solve real technical challenges and provide direct exposure to industry expectations.
